Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: [29/11/2025]

All Sales Are Final

We do not offer refunds or cancellations once an order is placed.

1. No Cancellation Policy

Once an order is successfully placed on our website, it cannot be cancelled or modified. Please review your order details, size, and shipping address carefully before completing your purchase.

2. No Refund Policy

We operate on a strict no-refund and no-return policy. We do not accept returns due to change of mind, sizing issues (please refer to our size chart before buying), or slight color variations due to screen resolution.

3. Damaged or Defective Items

In the unlikely event that you receive a damaged or incorrect product, we will offer a replacement (subject to stock availability) or store credit. You must:

  • Record an unboxing video clearly showing the package and the defect.
  • Notify us within 24 hours of delivery at [nooras.official@gmail.com].
  • Our team will verify the details and guide you through the exchange process if approved.
