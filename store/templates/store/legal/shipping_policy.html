Shipping and Delivery Policy

1. Courier Partner

We are proud to partner with DTDC Courier Services to ensure your order reaches you safely and on time.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Once your package is handed over to our courier partner, we will manually update your order status to 'Shipped'.

3. Delivery Timelines

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Rest of India: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days

Note: Delivery times may be affected by public holidays, weather conditions, or operational delays.

4. Order Tracking & Updates

We keep you informed at every step of the journey:

  • Email Notifications: You will receive an email notification as soon as your order status is updated to 'Shipped' by our team.
  • Website Status: You can check the live status of your order at any time by logging in and visiting the My Orders section on our website.
